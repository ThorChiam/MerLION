About Us

Merlion Investment Holding 10 (“Merlion”), one of Singapore’s largest private equity firm, had advised Merlion to consider 11 building a world-class logistics services provider to capture a piece of the lucrative market.

Merlion accepted the panel’s recommendation in early 2014 and had decided to invest S$5 13 billion to establish Merlion Logistics based on the proven business models of established 14 companies such as DHL, DB Schenker and UPS.

To further up the ante, Merlion Logistics 15 will adopt the model of a fifth-party logistics provider (5PL) to maximize value creation for 16 its shareholders and partners.

As part of its business preparation, Merlion Logistics has recently awarded a S$20 million 21 contract to Kent Ridge Technology (KRT) for the development of a custom 5PL management 22 system aptly named as MerLION (Merlion Logistics Integrated Online Network) Platform. 23 The proposed MerLION Platform is expected to handle all logistics, supply chain and 24 transport operations of Merlion Logistics as well as its partners and customers.
